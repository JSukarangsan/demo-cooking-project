# Discovery Synthesis Skill

Turns a pile of raw research into something a PM can act on.

## What I Need
- Raw research materials (paste or @ reference): transcripts, survey exports, notes, NPS comments
- The question or hypothesis the research was exploring (optional — I'll infer if not given)

## What I'll Produce
- `{topic}-synthesis.md` — themes, key insights, tensions and contradictions, signal vs. noise
- `{topic}-recommendations.md` — ranked recommendations with rationale, tied back to project goals

## How I Handle Different Input Types
- **Interview transcripts** — extract quotes, cluster by theme, surface the unexpected
- **Survey data** — identify patterns, flag outliers, separate what people say from what they do
- **NPS/support themes** — filter noise, find the signal behind surface complaints
- **Mixed inputs** — synthesize across sources, flag where they confirm or contradict each other
